🧘‍♂️ 5-Minute Meditation to Clear Your Mind

Feeling overwhelmed or distracted? A 5-minute meditation can work wonders. Even a short mindful pause can refresh your brain, calm your nerves, and bring back focus. Here's a simple guide anyone can follow — no experience required.

🌬️ Step 1: Find a Quiet Spot

Sit comfortably — on a chair or the floor. Make sure you’re away from noise and distractions. Close your eyes gently.

🧠 Step 2: Focus on Your Breath

  • Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 2 seconds
  • Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds

Repeat this for 5 rounds. Keep your attention on the breath — feel the air moving in and out.

🌀 Step 3: Let Thoughts Pass

If your mind wanders (and it will), gently bring it back to your breath. Don’t judge your thoughts — just observe and let them go like clouds passing by.

🔔 Step 4: Gently Return

After 5 minutes, slowly open your eyes. Stretch your hands. Smile. You’ve just reset your mind.

💡 Bonus Tip

Set a reminder to do this every morning or before stressful tasks. Just 5 minutes daily can improve clarity, reduce anxiety, and boost emotional health.

Start today — your mind deserves peace too.
