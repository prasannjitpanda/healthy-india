Healthy Banana Oats Smoothie

Need a quick and healthy breakfast? This Banana Oats Smoothie is rich in fiber, protein, and natural sweetness. Ready in under 5 minutes — it's the perfect way to kickstart your day!

📝 Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 4-5 ice cubes

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Add banana, oats, milk, honey, and cinnamon into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled texture.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy fresh!

💡 Tip:

Want extra protein? Add a scoop of peanut butter or protein powder.

Benefits:

This smoothie provides long-lasting energy, supports digestion, and keeps you full for hours. Plus, it's super easy and tasty!

Final Words:

Whether you're in a rush or just want something light yet filling — this banana oats smoothie is a great pick. Make it a part of your healthy routine.
